Echo

Bridging the gap between digital art discovery and authentic communal music experiences.

Project Overview

The Product

Echo is a community-driven platform for casual music enthusiasts seeking meaningful connections through curated content and aura-based matching.

Context

UXL Designathon 2025

The Team

A team of 4 designers collaborating on the 3 day designathon

My Role

UX Designer & Researcher

The Problem

In a digital age, technology has made art accessible but weakened artist-audience bonds. Users struggle to find like-minded individuals to attend local events with, fueling isolation and virtual disengagement.

The Goal

Echo aims to bridge this gap by helping individuals rediscover the joy of shared interests, fostering deeper artist-audience bonds, and promoting safe, seamless in-person engagement.

Understanding the Fanbase

We surveyed music fans in Creative and STEM fields to understand the barriers to fan interaction.

Target Market Insights

Social Barriers

The biggest barrier to interacting with fans is the difficulty in finding like-minded individuals within immediate networks.

Discovery Habits

While TikTok (26%) and Instagram (31%) dominate news, users crave the "Word of Mouth" experience for authentic bonding.

Desired Experience

A significant majority of users expressed a strong preference to bond in-person and attend local events together.

How Might We Statement

After analyzing user insights, we formulated a How Might We statement: "How might we foster an enriched community that encourages individuals to connect, share, and experience music in a safe & seamless way?"

The Echo Experience

Unlike traditional social media, Echo focuses on live events and tailored aura-based matching to facilitate authentic communal experiences.

Core Features

Aura-Based Matching

Connecting users through shared music "auras" to ensure compatibility in tastes and event preferences.

Event Discovery

A streamlined way to discover, plan, and engage with local happenings and niche artist showcases.

Network Expansion

Tools to find people both within and outside existing networks who share niche artistic interests.

Starting the Design

Low-Fidelity Prototypes

Initial sketches focused on intuitive navigation, clear event details, and seamless matching flows.

Visual Identity

The design utilizes high-contrast dark modes and vibrant "aura" colors to mimic the atmosphere of live music venues.

Product Walkthrough

Accessibility & Inclusion

Echo is built to be a safe and seamless space for all fans.

Safe Spaces

Verified community features to ensure in-person meetups are organized in safe, public, and moderated environments.

Visual Hierarchy

Clean typography and high-contrast UI elements to ensure event details are legible in low-light concert environments.

Inclusive Matching

Algorithms that prioritize interest-based connection over traditional social metrics to reduce exclusion.

Next Steps

  • Post-Event Integration: Provide features for users to share memories and photos within their new community groups.
  • Artist Collaboration: Partner with local artists to provide "Echo-Exclusive" ticket tiers and meet-and-greets.
  • Global Expansion: Scale the aura-matching algorithm to help fans find communities in new cities.
