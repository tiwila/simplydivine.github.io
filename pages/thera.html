Overview

TheraLink is a digital platform for teletherapy, offering a one-stop solution for individuals and families seeking mental health support. It connects users with licensed therapists and provides tools for managing their well-being.

Empathize

Research Goals

  • Identify pain points in finding therapists
  • Understand privacy, cost, and accessibility concerns
  • Explore what makes users feel comfortable vs intimidated

Research Summary

  • Users sought therapy mainly for anxiety and mental well-being
  • Many felt overwhelmed or hopeless
  • Barriers included cost, trust, and therapist fit

User Pain Points

  • High cost / lack of coverage
  • Difficulty matching with therapists
  • Privacy concerns
  • Low emotional connection online

User Personas

Alexia Thorn, 20

“Online therapy feels safer and less intimidating for me.”
  • Struggles with social anxiety
  • Prefers discreet, affordable online care

Mark Johnson, 33

  • Stressed software engineering lead

Define

Key Insights

Insight 1

Users feel overwhelmed by impersonal therapy platforms

Insight 2

Trust is built through transparency and credentials

Insight 3

Users want control over their mental health journey

How might we create an affordable, trustworthy teletherapy platform that feels emotionally supportive?
