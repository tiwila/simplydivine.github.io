TheraLink

A digital platform for teletherapy, offering a one-stop solution for mental health support.

Project Overview

The Product

TheraLink connects users with licensed therapists and provides tools for managing well-being.

Duration

June 2025 - August 2025

My Role

Lead UX Designer & Researcher

The Problem & Goal

Users struggle to find therapists matching their values, face high costs, and privacy concerns. TheraLink aims to make support accessible through an easy matching system and strong data privacy.

Understanding the User

I conducted user research to identify the hurdles users face in seeking digital mental health support.

Through interviews and surveys, I discovered that users often feel overwhelmed by the process of finding a suitable therapist, especially when considering factors like cost, compatibility, security concerns, and lack of emotional connection online.

Usability Study

Conducted usability testing with 5 participants to gather feedback on the low-fidelity prototype.

Key Findings

  • Streamlined Matching: Users appreciated the quick, intuitive therapist matching process.
  • Privacy Transparency: Initial confusion around data security led to clearer privacy badge placements.
  • Personalized Curation: Users desired more specific filter options for therapist identity and values.

Accessibility Considerations

Color & Contrast

Ensure sufficient color contrast and avoid using color alone to convey information, supporting users with low vision and color blindness.

Keyboard Nav

Ensure all buttons, forms, and links are fully keyboard-accessible with clear visual focus indicators.

Screen Reader Compatibility

Use semantic HTML and descriptive alt text to ensure screen reader users can easily understand and navigate the site.
